About This Site

After every Kings win, the beam is lit. The physical beam shoots from Golden 1 Center into the Sacramento sky after home wins, but for Kings fans everywhere, the beam is a state of mind. When the Kings win, the beam is lit until the next game. During live games, the site shows win probability — the answer changes from YES to MAYBE to UNLIKELY as the game progresses. All data is pulled from ESPN's public API. Scores update every 30 seconds during live games. Season archives go back to 2022-23 when the beam tradition began.
