First Lit · 2022

A 1.5 million candlepower searchlight on the roof of Golden 1 Center. Fires after every Kings win — home or road. Stays lit about an hour. Visible from miles across the valley.

Started October 29, 2022 — the night the Kings beat the Heat 119-113 at home, kicking off the season they'd end a 16-year playoff drought. The beam became the rallying cry.

"Light the beam" outgrew basketball. Got a promotion? Light the beam. Kid took their first steps? Light the beam. It's a Sacramento thing.

This site tracks every beam. Data via ESPN's public API. Scores update every 30 seconds during live games. Season archives back to 2022-23.

Not affiliated with the Sacramento Kings. Made by a fan, for fans.
