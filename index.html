Sacramento, California

FYBA Legend

After every Kings win, the beam is lit. The physical beam shoots from Golden 1 Center into the Sacramento sky after home wins, but for Kings fans everywhere, the beam is a state of mind. Win a game, light the beam. The tradition started in 2022-23 when the Kings ended a 16-year playoff drought.

All data is pulled from ESPN's public API. Scores update every 30 seconds during live games, every 2 minutes otherwise. Win probability is pulled from ESPN's game summary. Season archives go back to 2022-23 when the beam tradition began. The site auto-rolls to the new season each October.
